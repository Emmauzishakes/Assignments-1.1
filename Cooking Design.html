HOW TO COOK UGALI, FISH STEW & SUKUMA RECIPE

Ugali

Ingredients

  • 1. 1 Cup flour (more or less)
  • 2. 2 Cups water (more or less)

    • Cooking Instructions

  • 1. Boil water until it bubbles. The water should be very hot. This ensures the ugali will cook perfectly
  • 2. Add a cup full of flour into the water. Let it cook a few seconds until water starts to cover the flour
  • 3. Use a wooden spoon to quickly start mixing the flour and water
  • 4. Add a handful of flour and continue to mix the water and flour it will start to come together
  • 5. Reduce heat to medium and keep turning the ugali as it continues to stick together
  • 6. Gather the ugali and press it onto the side of the sufuria
  • 7. Place the wooden spoon under the ugali and form into a ball in the middle of the sufuria
  • 8. Repeat pressing the ugali on the side of the sufuria and turning it to the middle
  • 9. Once the ugali has become firm, turn it once more and smoothen it into a round. You will know your ugali is ready once you start to smell the aroma clearly
  • 10. Turn the ugali over onto a plate
  • 11. Serve the ugali whole or slice. Enjoy with your veggies & fish

    • Fish stew

    Ingredients

  • 1 medium tilapia fish(deep fried), medium tomatoes (chopped), 1 small onion, 1 big clove of galic, Salt, Cooking oil,1 slice lemon

    • Cooking Instructions

  • 1. In a pot chop you onion and fry for about 2mins then add your garlic fry till golden brown........now add your chopped tomatoes and salt
  • 2. Let the tomatoes cook well for about 4mins then add some little water for the stew.....let it boil for 2mins
  • 3. Aside sprinkle lemon drops juice in both sides of your fish to enhance the taste and keep off fish smell......now add your fish to tomato soup....let it boil well for about 6mins to create that stew very well
  • 4. When done now serve with accompaniment of your choice

    • Sukuma(kales)

    Ingredients

  • 1. 1 bunch of Kales washed and chopped
  • 2. 1 small onion
  • 3. 1/2 table spoon oil
  • 4. Chili (optional)
  • 5. 2 garlic cloves minced

    • Cooking Instructions

  • 1. Prepare the ingredients
  • Slice the onions and grate garlic,
  • Next heat your cooking pot under a medium heat, add cooking oil, anion and minced garlic cloves then stir to cook for 2 to 3 minutes, once the garlic turns brown add in Kales.
  • 2. You can add seasonings like royco cubes or salt to test, top it with little chilli as well.
  • Serve with Fish Stew & ugali.

    • Serve and enjoy your meal
